Website Design - Overview

Website Design, Building & Development in Puerto Rico

Located in Puerto Rico, Fenn Design can get your business on the internet with a meticulously designed Web Site that will communicate and project the desired image to the desired audience. We are web site designers, builders, developers and provide web site hosting in Puerto Rico...but there is a difference.

Marketing & Advertising Experience

Fenn Design founder and owner, Peter Fenn, brings 35 years of advertising and marketing knowledge to the table; we aren’t just techies and pretty pictures, but professional communicators and our company is small enough that nothing goes “out the door” which hasn’t been thoroughly reviewed by Mr. Fenn. Being located in Puerto Rico means that we are also well positioned for marketing to Hispanic targets.

Award Winning Graphic Design

Fenn Design is well known in Puerto Rico as one of the leading design studios. The firm has won many design accolades from the advertising and publishing industries. When designing a website, we pay attention to the details. The “Look & Feel” of a website creates the initial visitor’s perception of your business, in his or her mind. That perception is created from font selection, color palette, typography formatting and copywriting and imagery.

Professional Copywriting (English & Spanish)

Fenn Design uses experienced, professional editors and copywriters to create the written content of each website. We are proficient in the Spanish and English idioms.

Search Engine Experts

Since 1994 when two Stanford University students in a campus trailer created Yahoo, we at Fenn Design have been studying the evolving technology of Search Engines. Because of our understanding of how search engines work, all of our website clients enjoy excellent page rankings in today’s most popular search engines.

What good is a website presenting your products and services if your potential customers can’t find it? 

Website Hosting & email Support

Fenn Design websites are hosted on state-of-he-art, dedicated servers. All hosted websites are backed up to remote hard drives weekly. And for those green minded websites we also have a server powered by solar and wind energy!

email support issues available by phone working hours, Monday thru Friday.
